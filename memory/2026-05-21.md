## Doc 6 - Race Director's Competition Notes (17:25, Rui Marques)
Critical notes for the weekend:

1. **Delta time enforcement** between Safety Car lines during SQ/Quali/Recon laps
2. **Parc Fermé access routes** defined with diagrams
3. **Lapping:** Blue flags at 1.2s, pre-warning at 3.0s
4. **Article B5.13.6:** No erratic acceleration/braking before safety car restart
5. **ERS Safety Check** after covers off each morning
6. **Pit Lane Safety:** Personnel only allowed immediately before work, must withdraw after
7. **LTCS/TTCs:** Only completed lap times count for classification
8. **Starting Procedure:** Blue lights flash 5s, "Pre-Start" message, then light sequence
9. **DAS:** Enabled AFTER Turn 1 (not at race start!)
10. **Finish Line:** Control Line on track (not pit lane)
11. **Double waved yellow:** Lap time deleted in FP
12. **Pit entry/exit marshalling:** Subject to sector flags until passing blue line
13. **Support races:** Barrier max 3m from garage, release 20 mins before session
14. **Practice starts:** Only after pit exit, left-hand grid boxes, not during SQ or Quali
15. **Article B1.6.3d:** "In the fast lane" = all of tyre beyond white line separating fast/inner lane
16. **Pit lane is NARROW** - released cars must blend without impeding
17. **Pit entry/exit lines:** Stay LEFT of solid white line on right-hand side
18. **Quali interruption:** <70 seconds remaining = no resumption
19. **Weighing:** Top 10 must go directly to scales, no drinking/weight increase
20. **ERS Hazard:** Mechanics to pit exit area, picked up by car
21. **Garage exit:** No pit lane entry until 5 mins before session start
22. **Safety Car resumption:** Leaves pit lane 1 min before, waits before Turn 8
23. **Grid definition:** From front of grid box #1 to rear of grid box #30
24. **Circuit changes:** Smoother kerb T1, drainage T2, blue line T2 exit, wall realignments T6/T13/T14, openings with gates in pit wall
25. **Detection line:** Same as SC Line 1, OT boards both sides
26. **FP1 grip messages:** Low Grip Delta → Low Grip Conditions, then Normal Grip after 3 cars reach Turn 9
27. **Track Limits (CRITICAL):**
    - T1: Fail = rejoin at far end of runoff
    - T9: Pass completely left of orange apex kerb = keep left, rejoin far end
    - T13: Pass completely left of orange apex kerb = keep left of orange line on exit, rejoin far end
    - **T13/T14: FAIL = THAT LAP AND THE NEXT LAP INVALIDATED!** (31.4)
